Shih-tzu

Breed from Asia

Nickname: Lion Dog

History of Shih Tzu:

  • 800 B.C. - hence the name "Lion Dog"
  • 1930 - The first dogs of the breed were imported into Europe (England and Norway)
  • 1935 - The first European standard for the breed was written in England
  • 1950 - Soliders from Europe, took this breed to US
  • 1969 - The Shih Tzu was recognized by the American Kennel Club
  • 2013 - United States, the Shih Tzu ranked the 15th most popular breed
  • 2015 - Popularity of Shih Tzu goes down

"The name comes from the Chinese language word for "lion" because this kind of dog was bred to resemble the lion as depicted in traditional oriental art"
